About Us

Welcome to Vidya Vikas Society College of Arts, Science & Commerce Sports Day! Our annual Sports Day celebrates athleticism, teamwork, and school spirit. With a variety of exciting individual and team sports, including chess, badminton, kabaddi, and volleyball, this event is a chance for students and faculty to come together and showcase their talent. Whether you're here to compete or cheer, Sports Day brings our college community closer, fostering unity and sportsmanship. Join us in making this a memorable day of fun, competition, and camaraderie! ann
